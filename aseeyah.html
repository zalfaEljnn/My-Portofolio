Story Behind the Name

Why “Aseeyah"?

Named after Asiyah, the wife of the Pharaoh, remembered in tradition for her unwavering faith and devotion to the One God despite great trials.

We honour her courage — the quiet conviction to stand by truth when everything around you insists otherwise.

Wearing Aseeyah is a reminder that steadfast faith can be gentle and beautifully dignified.
