Story Behind the Name

Why “Fateema"?

Inspired by Fatimah az-Zahra, a woman of purity, patience, and strength.

We wanted every wearer to feel wrapped in calmness — the kind of serenity she carried through every trial.

Wearing Fateema means choosing softness without fragility, and strength without harshness.

Find your calm in Fateema.
Because modesty will always be beautiful.
